How to Get the New Google Bar With a Cookie Tweak

Dec 3rd, 2011

I’ve seen a blog post on Lifehacker about getting the new Google Bar. It has some pointless shit on installing a Google Chrome extension, that is really unnecessary! You can just do it via the console on the browser.

This only seems to work for google.com domain as it is rolling out onthat site first.

It works on all google domains. I was being stupid and not reading the code properly. In the little bit of code that you copy and paste change .google.com to your Google, so I would change it to .google.co.uk and follow the other steps as usual. It works on ALL Google!

To enable the new Google Bar on Google Chrome:

  1. Press Shift + Ctrl + J.
  2. Copy and paste 

To enable the new Google Bar on Firefox:

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + K.
  2. Copy and paste 

To enable the new Google Bar on Internet Explorer:

  1. Press F12.
  2. Click the Console tab.
  3. Copy and paste 

To enable the new Google Bar on Safari:

  1. Press Ctrl + ,.
  2. Click Advanced tab.
  3. Check “Show Develop in menu bar”.
  4. Close window.
  5. Press Ctrl + Alt + C.
  6. Copy and paste 

To enable the new Google Bar on Opera:

Note: As ryandvm of HackerNews posted, it doesn’t work for Google Apps account yet.
